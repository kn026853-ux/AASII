WHO WE ARE

Engineering Excellence

AS Infrastructure & Survey Solution Engineering is a leading engineering consultancy and infrastructure service provider delivering innovative, reliable, and high-quality solutions across India.

We specialize in highway engineering, traffic surveys, DPR preparation, pavement investigation, FWD surveys, topographic surveys, and project management consultancy services. With a team of experienced professionals and advanced survey technologies, we are committed to providing accurate data, efficient project execution, and cost-effective engineering solutions for roads, highways, bridges, and infrastructure development projects.
